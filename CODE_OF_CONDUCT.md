# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors.

---

## Centralized Code of Conduct

Kubevuln follows the Kubescape project's centralized Code of Conduct.

**👉 [View the centralized CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](https://github.com/kubescape/project-governance/blob/main/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)**

---

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of:

- Age
- Body size
- Disability
- Ethnicity
- Sex characteristics
- Gender identity and expression
- Level of experience
- Education
- Socio-economic status
- Nationality
- Personal appearance
- Race
- Religion
- Sexual identity and orientation

---

## Our Standards

### Positive Behaviors

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- ✅ Using welcoming and inclusive language
- ✅ Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- ✅ Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- ✅ Focusing on what is best for the community
- ✅ Showing empathy towards other community members
- ✅ Helping newcomers learn and contribute

### Unacceptable Behaviors

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- ❌ The use of sexualized language or imagery
- ❌ Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- ❌ Public or private harassment
- ❌ Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- ❌ Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

---

## Enforcement

### Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by:

1. **Email**: conduct@kubescape.io
2. **GitHub**: Report via repository security tab
3. **Slack**: DM a maintainer

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

### Response

Project maintainers are obligated to:

- Maintain confidentiality of the reporter
- Review and investigate reports promptly
- Take appropriate action based on the circumstances

### Consequences

Community impact guidelines for enforcement:

| Level | Behavior | Consequence |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| 1. Correction | Unprofessional or unwelcome behavior | Private warning |
| 2. Warning | A violation through a series of actions | Temporary interaction ban |
| 3. Temporary Ban | Serious or sustained inappropriate behavior | Temporary ban from community spaces |
| 4. Permanent Ban | Pattern of violations or severe harassment | Permanent ban |
